About US

Our seafarer brethren excels not only at sea but also in any other field which they ventures in. And one such initiative which we would love to introduce to you all is Envision Medical Imaging & Scan Centre which is one of the best DG Shipping approved Medical Centre at Patna being run by joint teams of able medical professionals and Marine offices like Capt. Ashok Kumar, Capt. Pramod Kumar and Chief Officer Santosh Kumar.

The Multi-Speciality Diagnostic Centre located in easily approachable Prime location in Kankarbagh, Patna, is equipped with state-of-art fully digitalized diagnostic machines, and is manned by highly qualified and experienced team of medical professionals under able guidance of its revered directors Dr. Ajay Kumar Lal, Radiologist, Dr. Shamsul Hoda, Orthopaedic Surgeon and Dr. Mohit Kumar, Dental Surgeon.

It provides medical test to all seafarers under one roof without any delay and disturbance, and proudly boasts of having done the medical tests for reputed companies like Destan Ship Management, NYK Company, SCI, Blue Bird Ship Management, Shin Yang Shipping, Macroni Ship Management, BHN Offshore Pvt Ltd, just to name a few.
